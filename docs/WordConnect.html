Privacy Policy

Overview

This Privacy Policy outlines how WordConnect collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our game. It also explains your rights regarding your personal information and how you can contact us with any questions or concerns.

Introduction

At WordConnect, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the circumstances under which we may share it. By using WordConnect, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Personal Information We Collect

WordConnect does not require user login, so we do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number directly from you. However, through the use of third-party services like Unity Ads, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Information about your interactions with the game and ads

How We Use Your Personal Information

The information collected through Unity Ads is used to:

  • Serve personalized advertisements
  • Analyze user interactions to improve the game experience
  • Support in-app purchases and other game features

Who We Share Your Personal Information With

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information with:

  • Third-party service providers like Unity Ads for advertising purposes
  • App stores such as Google Play or Apple App Store for processing in-app purchases

Where We Process Your Personal Information

Your personal information may be processed outside of your country of residence, including in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate. By using WordConnect, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

Contact & Complaints

If you have any questions, concerns, or complaints about this Privacy Policy or our handling of your personal information, please contact us at ideapetal@gmail.com. We will do our best to address your concerns in a timely and satisfactory manner.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the latest revision will be indicated at the top of the policy. Your continued use of WordConnect after any changes signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.

Advertising and User Acquisition Partners

We partner with third-party advertisers and user acquisition partners to promote WordConnect. These partners may collect and use information about your interactions with our game to serve targeted ads or analyze the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

Children's Privacy

Our Games are not targeted at children. Where Children under 16 use our Games, we advise that they obtain the permission of a parent or guardian, and we recommend that parents or guardians clear their browser cache of cookies and take all other steps highlighted in this policy to minimise the data collected.

Unity Ads

We use Unity Ads to serve advertisements in WordConnect. Unity Ads may collect data such as your device identifier, IP address, and information about your interactions with the ads to provide personalized advertisements. Unity's privacy policy can be found at Unity Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

WordConnect offers in-app purchases to enhance your gaming experience. When you make a purchase, the transaction is processed through the app store (e.g., Google Play or Apple App Store). We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to the privacy policy of your app store for more details:

  • Google Play Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store Privacy Policy

No User Login Required

WordConnect does not require any user login. You can enjoy the game without creating an account or providing any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
