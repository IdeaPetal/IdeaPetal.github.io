Privacy Policy for Word Connect: Journey of Mind

Effective Date: 14th Sept 2024

Thank you for playing Word Connect: Journey of Mind. Your privacy is important to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our game.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

Word Connect: Journey of Mind does not require you to create an account or log in to play the game. We do not collect any personal information, such as your name, email address, or contact details, while you use the app.

1.2 Non-Personal Information

While playing Word Connect: Journey of Mind, certain non-personal information may be automatically collected to improve user experience and ensure smooth gameplay. This includes:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Game performance data, such as game progress and usage statistics
  • Unique device identifiers (such as IDFA or Android Advertising ID)

1.3 In-App Purchases

If you make an in-app purchase, your transaction will be processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not have access to your payment information. The stores may collect personal data as part of the transaction process. Please review the respective store's privacy policies for more information:

  • Apple App Store Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Store Privacy Policy

1.4 Unity Ads

Word Connect: Journey of Mind uses Unity Ads to display advertisements. Unity Ads may collect information such as:

  • Ad interaction data (e.g., whether you viewed or clicked on an ad)
  • Device identifiers and usage information

For more details on Unity Ads' data collection practices, please refer to Unity's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the non-personal information collected for the following purposes:

  • To improve game performance and user experience
  • To understand player behavior and usage patterns
  • To serve relevant advertisements and optimize ad performance
  • To enable in-app purchases

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. However, we may share non-personal information with third-party service providers, such as Unity Ads, for the purpose of displaying ads within the game.

4. Children's Privacy

Word Connect: Journey of Mind is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at ideapetal@gmail.com, and we will take steps to delete such information.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. Please review this page periodically to stay informed about our data practices.
