Support for WordConnect

How to Play

In WordConnect, you connect the characters provided in each puzzle to form valid words. Your goal is to solve each puzzle by finding all the words hidden within the given characters.

In-App Purchases

You can purchase coins in WordConnect using real money. These coins can be used to unlock premium features and hints to help you solve difficult puzzles.

To make an in-app purchase:

  • Go to the in-game store.
  • Select the coin package you want to purchase.
  • Confirm the purchase via your device's app store (Google Play or Apple App Store).
