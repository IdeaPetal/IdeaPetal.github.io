Word Connect: Journey of Mind Support

Welcome to the Word Connect: Journey of Mind Support page! Here, we provide all the information you need to enjoy and get the most out of the game. If you encounter any issues or have any questions, we’re here to help.

Game Overview

Word Connect: Journey of Mind is a fun and engaging word puzzle game where players connect given characters to create valid words and solve puzzles. Challenge yourself with progressively harder levels, each designed to test your vocabulary and puzzle-solving skills!

Key Features:

  • No Account Required: Jump right into the action! Word Connect: Journey of Mind does not require you to create an account or log in to play.
  • In-App Purchases: You can buy consumable coins, which are used to unlock special features and hints to help you solve challenging puzzles.
  • Unity Ads: The game includes ads that you can watch to gain extra rewards or advance in the game.

Coins and In-App Purchases

Coins are the currency in Word Connect: Journey of Mind and are used to unlock hints or special features that help solve puzzles. You can obtain coins in the following ways:

  • Purchases: Coins can be bought via in-app purchases.
  • Watching Ads: Occasionally, you can earn extra coins by watching optional ads.

Coins are consumable items, meaning they are used up when you spend them on hints or features. They cannot be refunded once used. All purchases are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play, depending on your device.

No Login Required

Word Connect: Journey of Mind is designed to be simple and accessible. You don’t need to create an account or log in to play. Your progress is stored locally on your device. However, this means that if you uninstall the app or change devices, your progress and purchased coins will not transfer to the new device.

Troubleshooting and FAQs

1. I lost my coins after reinstalling the app.

Since Word Connect: Journey of Mind does not require an account, progress and purchases are stored locally on your device. If the app is deleted or your device is changed, coins and game progress may be lost. Unfortunately, we are unable to restore lost coins or progress.

2. I didn't receive my coins after a purchase.

If you made an in-app purchase and didn’t receive your coins, please:

  • Make sure your payment was successfully processed.
  • Restart the game to refresh your coin balance.
  • If the issue persists, feel free to reach out to us via email at ideapetal@gmail.com.

3. Why am I seeing ads?

Word Connect: Journey of Mind includes ads as part of the free game experience. Watching ads may provide you with rewards such as additional coins, which can help you advance in the game.

4. Can I transfer my progress to another device?

Since Word Connect: Journey of Mind doesn’t use accounts or cloud saving, progress is tied to your device. Unfortunately, it’s not possible to transfer progress or coins to a new device.
